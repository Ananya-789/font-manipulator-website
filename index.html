WELCOME TO - FONT MANIPULATOR!

INSTRUCTIONS :

1.This web application can change the font size of a text by use of the left wrist and right wrist.
2. Closer the left wrist and right wrist to each other, smaller the size.
3.Farther the left wrist and right wrist from each other,larger the size.And the text will be nothing but the user's name!
